FAQ
Common questions
How is this different from buying a lead database?
A lead database gives you names and emails — cold, untargeted, often outdated. We find real businesses currently active in your city, score them for fit, and write a message specifically about their company. The outreach is warm and relevant, not a blast.
Do I need to know anything technical?
No. You tell us your ideal customer (industry + city), approve messages before they go out, and receive replies in your inbox. The AI handles everything in between.
Is WhatsApp outreach legal and safe?
Yes. We use the official WhatsApp Business API (via Gupshup). All messages are sent through verified business channels. We also manage opt-out requests automatically to stay compliant.
What if I don't like a message before it sends?
Every single message goes into an approval queue before sending. You can edit, approve, or reject each one. Nothing goes out without your sign-off.
How long before I see meetings?
Most clients see first replies within 5–7 days of first send. Meetings typically start booking in week 2–3. The Day 3 and Day 7 follow-ups significantly increase conversion rates.
What industries does this work for?
We've seen strong results in SaaS, IT staffing, real estate, EdTech, marketing agencies, and B2B services. The free audit will tell you your market size and expected results before you commit.
